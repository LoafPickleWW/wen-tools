Algorand NFT Standards & Minting Tools

  • ARC-3 Minting: The standard for immutable Algorand NFTs. Bind your digital art to the ledger with permanent IPFS links.
  • ARC-19 Minting: Advanced evolvable NFTs. Update your metadata CID via the Asset Reserve field while maintaining decentralized integrity.
  • ARC-69 Minting: Lightweight, efficient NFTs that store metadata in transaction notes. Ideal for high-volume social collections.
  • Simple Batch Mint: Mass-mint hundreds of assets in minutes with automated IPFS pinning via Crust and Pinata.
  • Really Simple Mint: A minimalist one-click minting experience for creators who need speed and simplicity.
